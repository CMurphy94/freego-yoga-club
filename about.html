Freego was founded in 2022 with the goal of making Yoga free and accessible to all. We believe in the many physical, health and spiritual benefits that come with the practice of Yoga.

Yoga is something that should be enjoyed by all and we believe this should not be prevented by a lack a funds.

All that we ask is that you bring a yoga matt with you along with some water and comfortable clothing to work out it.
We'll take care of the rest.
